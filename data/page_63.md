# Why partner with GitHub?
Discover a variety of partnership opportunities with GitHub that cater to different aspects of business growth and technical collaboration. Whether you're looking to integrate your technology with GitHub's platform, enhance educational initiatives, support startups, or engage in channel services, we offer tailored programs to meet your needs. Explore how you can leverage GitHub's resources, network, and expertise to achieve your goals.
![a logo representing connection](https://github.githubassets.com/assets/technology-partners-780755984a02.png)
## Technology Partnership
Transform the way software is built, deployed, and maintained. Partner with us to deliver innovative user experiences within your product and on GitHub.
[Learn more and Apply](https://github.com/partners/technology-partners)
![a logo representing services](https://github.githubassets.com/assets/services-and-partners-9486e071ea62.png)
## Channel and Services Partnership
Partner with GitHub to expand your team's capabilities, grow your pipeline, and become a trusted advisor for your customers. Get sales, technical, and marketing resources along with tailored enablement from our experienced teams to help you grow your business.
[Learn more and Apply](https://github.com/partners/service-channel-partners)
![icon of a training session](https://github.githubassets.com/assets/training-c201076d7d81.png)
## Education Partnership
Partner with us to shape the next generation of developers. Through innovative collaborations, resource sharing, product integrations, and engaging events, we create opportunities that inspire and educate learners worldwide. Discover how you can get involved and make a lasting impact.
[Learn more and Apply](https://github.com/education/partners)
